Hi fellow PhD researchers!

We are looking for PhD buddies!!!

Starting a PhD is no easy thing, especially if you’re moving to Berlin from a different country or region. Often all you need is someone to answer your PhD-related questions and give you some friendly advice. Who better to help you with the PhD transition than people who have just been through the same thing?!

Introducing the PhD buddy system! New students (who request a buddy) are matched with a volunteer PhD student who they can contact or meet up with for newcomer-related advice.
The goal:
  • Provide a friendly face on campus for newcomers
  • A contact for advice on starting a PhD, working at the MDC or moving to Berlin
  • Informal meetups in your own time
Do you remember what it was like starting a PhD? Is there anything you’d like to have known? What advice would you give your past first-year self?
Help others by becoming a buddy!

All you have to do is fill in and send the attached form to anita.waltho@mdc-berlin.de

Matches will be main primarily on your institute location, butother information helps us make a closer match if possible. Have any questions or feedback? Please contact us!
