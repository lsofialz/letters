Dear PhD researchers,!


The Assembly is next week and the candidates that will represent you and make your PhD a better experience next year are listed below. What´s missing? That you vote on 2 of them on the 12th December!

Thank you so much guys for stepping forward- lets continue to push!

Ash (Buch)

My main motivation lies in the aspect of mental health with a sub-focus on diversity/international students - specially "third culture kids".

I want to open dialogue and bridge connections with these issues in mind - because knowing you are definitely not the only one with certain struggles, and having people to talk this through and form support networks with can help us all ultimately become happier individuals and secondarily, better scientists.

Olya(Buch)

My motivation is the phrase “be the change you want to see in the world”, or in this case “be the change you want to see in the MDC”

As a newbie PhD student, it’s important for me to help make those years here at MDC pleasant and fruitful.

Kerstin(Buch)

I would like to help PhD students to feel more as part of a community by organizing events such as the PhD retreat and maybe reviving the Beer Hour in Buch. Moreover, I would like to help expanding training possibilities.

Jacobo (Buch)

Healthy mind, fruitful research, proudly PhD. Building a sense of belonging; even though a PhD seems a lonely journey, we fellow students should team up for our own mental health and the wealth of our research.

Somesh (BIMSB)

I would like to become a PhD representative to serve as a liaison between the administration and the PhD students, and to try and increase the participation and engagement of PhD students themselves in activities relating to PhD representatives.
