MDC | PhD Representatives

Doing a PhD can be really challenging. Not only scientifically, but also personally as we need to deal with constant failure, impostor syndrome, failed expectations and just feeling exhausted. At the same time, we grow and learn a lot very fast, which together with stressful situations from the lab and outside sometimes can make it hard to cope.
We, PhD reps, feel this constantly and would like to share it with you. We know all PhD students have these feelings at some point and it is hard to make the best out of them.
In order to open the discussion about this, we will do a mental health awareness month starting April. What this includes: about 6 emails about this topic, a Pizza lunch with a discussion and hopefully some information about the new appointed therapist for the MDC and testimonies of people that are in therapy nowadays
